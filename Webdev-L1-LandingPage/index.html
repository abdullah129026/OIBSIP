Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

A lean, token-driven system engineered for consistency across every surface.

  • Semantic Tokens

    Color, spacing, typography, and radius tokens that map directly to your brand — no raw hex values, no guesswork.

  • Accessible by Default

    WCAG 2.2 AA compliance baked in. Focus-visible states, contrast checks, and keyboard navigation on every component.

  • Responsive Layouts

    Every component defines behavior across breakpoints — from mobile to ultra-wide — with edge-case handling included.

  • State Management

    Default, hover, focus-visible, active, disabled, loading, and error — every state documented and implementation-ready.

  • Dark Mode Ready

    Semantic surface and text tokens flip seamlessly between light and dark contexts with zero overrides needed.

  • Component Anatomy

    Every component ships with defined anatomy, variants, interaction patterns, and anti-patterns for your team.

About Essence

Design systems,
done right

Essence was founded on a simple belief: every team deserves a design system that works as hard as they do. We build token-driven, accessible systems that scale with your product — not against it.

  • Consistency first System-level decisions over one-off overrides, every time.
  • Accessibility always WCAG 2.2 AA is the floor, not the ceiling.
  • Implementation ready Every spec ships with testable acceptance criteria.
  • Team ownership Systems your team can maintain without us in the room.
